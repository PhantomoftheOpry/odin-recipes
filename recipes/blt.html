Bacon, Lettuce, and Tomato

Home

Description

As a kid I HATED 1. Lettuce and 2. Tomatoes. I LOVED Bacon. As I grew older I grew to like vegetables and would eat lettuce and I tolerated tomatoes. That is, until I found Duke's Mayonnaise. Once Duke's came into my life, I eat a BLT for dinner nearly every week.

Ingredients

  • 1 Head of Lettuce, rinsed
  • 1 Tomato, sliced
  • 1 lb of Bacon
  • Duke's Mayonnaise
  • Bread of your choice
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook bacon to your desired doneness, place on plate with paper towel to drain grease
  2. Toast bread to your liking
  3. Slather a healthy dose of Duke's mayonnaise on one...or both slices of Toast
  4. Layer 1-2 leaves of lettuce, 1-2 slices of tomatoe, and as much bacon as you like on the toast
  5. ENJOY
  6. Make Another
